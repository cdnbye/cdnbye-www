    Data Analysis

    SwarmCloud

    CNDBye Dashboard provides quick overview of real-time and historical p2p statistics of your website/app.

    # Realtime Data

    Here, you can see how many users online currently, how many playback sessions today and how much your current p2p offload is, and so on.

    • Current Online Viewers
    • P2P Traffic Today
    • Playback Sessions Today
    • Remaining Traffic
    • Peak Concurrent Viewers
    • Pie chart(Version, Customer Label, Terminal, Live/VOD, Network type, ISP, NAT type)
    • Content Popularity

    # Historical Data

    Historical Data section allows you to see traffic history and online viewers statistics. You can identify the prime time of your service and see traffic usage over time.

    • P2P/HTTP Traffic Trend
    • Online Viewers Trend
    • Rebuffer Ratio Trend

    # Viewer Geographical Distribution

    Using the geographical distribution reports section you can see how many users you have depending on the different geographical region.
    Please note: SwarmCloud does not use precise location by default and therefore only general regions are shown.

    • Global Distribution
    • USA Distribution
    • China Distribution
