Whether you provide Media-player technologies or Video-Streaming verticals; CDN services or Technology selling, we at SwarmCloud invite you for partnership. SwarmCloud amplifies any streaming product with extra advantages of decentralized content delivery and provides outstanding streaming quality to end clients.

# For video platforms & streaming services

As an easy-to-implement solution, SwarmCloud complements your current proposal for content streamers.
Whether listed as an item in your solutions marketplace or as a part of your platform, when SwarmCloud is pre-integrated, any client could easily use it for CDN amplification.

# For Media-player developers

SwarmCloud allows embedding the function of distributed content delivery in any JS media player and native player. We will share with you the revenue from its traffic costs.

# For system integrators

SwarmCloud's signature destination is to build and sell best-in-class video solutions to revolutionize video delivery over the web and make it available for any media customer everywhere.
If you have strong knowledge of Broadcasting market in your region and already support technical solutions for this vertical, we'll be glad to contact you.
